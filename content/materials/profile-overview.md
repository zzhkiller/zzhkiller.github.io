# Zhouxing Zhao - Public Profile Overview

Zhouxing Zhao is an R&D Director and Chief Architect based in Hangzhou, China. His work focuses on AI engineering, data governance, low-altitude UAV applications, cloud-edge collaboration, heterogeneous computing, and intelligent scheduling systems.

He currently works at Zhejiang Communications Services Co., Ltd. His public profile combines enterprise architecture, engineering management, research-oriented product development, and digital transformation delivery.

Research and engineering interests include computer vision, large model applications, data governance, cloud-edge-device collaboration, low-altitude economy scenarios, UAV scheduling, DevOps platforms, knowledge graphs, and heterogeneous computing resource scheduling.
